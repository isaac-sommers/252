How to Play

  • Players alternate turns. Each turn: roll two dice and place both numbers on any two adjacent empty squares — or cash a block instead.
  • A block is any solid rectangle of your own squares, at least 2×2. Cashing scores the sum of its numbers and ends your turn (no roll).
  • Blocks are worth double if they contain a doubles roll (✦), and double again if they cover 8 or more squares — up to ×4 total.
  • First to 252 points wins. If Player 1 hits 252, Player 2 gets one final turn to tie.
